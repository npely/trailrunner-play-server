TrailRunner

Rules
  • TrailRunner is a logic-based puzzle game.
  • The objective is to escape the dungeon without dying.
  • Each field in the dungeon has a number on it, which represents the times you are allowed to step on the specific field.
  • Every time you leave the field the number decreases.
  • Once the field reaches 0, it breaks and you are not allowed to step back onto it. If you do, you lose.
  • The door to the dungeon only opens once every field is broken, so you need to think about every step, to not block your own way and still make your way out!

There are many different routes you can use in the URL to play the game:

/level = leads you to the level selection
/level/id = adding an id to the level selection leads you to the specified level
/move = with the move route you can speciy all the different moves
/move/up = lets the player move one field up
/move/down = lets the player move one field down
/move/left = lets the player move one field left
/move/right = lets the player move one field right
/move/undo = undoes the last step the player took
/move/redo = redoes the last step the player undid

We developed this game and the idea behind it ourselves. We tried to come up with something original, which was still able to fulfill all the tasks given to us in the course Software Engineering.
